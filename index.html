Nikita Dagar

Software Engineer | UI/UX Designer


I'm currently a Computer Science grad student at the University of Florida.
I'm a part of the Intelligent Natural Interaction Technology lab advised by Dr. Lisa Anthony where I'm exploring how we can motivate children to be more active through video games, using full body gesture recognition such as Microsoft Kinect. Previously, I worked at Digital Green as an Assistant Software Engineer and got my bachelor's degree at IIIT - Delhi.
I love building technology that makes people's lives easier. I'm particularly interested in Human Computer Interaction and Natural User Interfaces.
